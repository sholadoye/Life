Initial Stage - Infancy

It is as it implies, the initial (start) stage of any process, where in this case is the early life of an individual when all decisions is being taken for him or her. It is a time of ignorance

Middle Stage - Mid-age

Is the time of decision making, when an individual is accountable for his or her decision making. This is a crucial time when decisions are weighed. i.e this is the time whe you decide to be productive or not

Final Stage - Old-age

This is the time of reckoning, flashbacks and accountability. This is when a man looks back at plans, actions, decisions,
